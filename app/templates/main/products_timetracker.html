Physician Time Tracker

Products

Tracking physician arrangement times couldn't be easier

TimeTracker consists of the web application, iPhone app, and Android app to track physician times, avoid compliance issues, and allow for incredibly easy auditing



Within a physician arrangement, doctors are contractually obligated to work for a certain number of hours. While many systems attempt to keep track of this, they do it in inefficent, and expensive ways. With TimeTracker, you can have an all digital solution that doctors will love to use and keep you compliant.

How It Works

Our Simple Process

1

Physicians enter times in phone app or on web

With our focus on simplicity and a great user experience, physicians will be able to enter times with a breeze!
2

Approval Process Takes Place
