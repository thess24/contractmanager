Create, Manage, and Track Physician Arrangement Contracts

ContractManager is a SaaS tool to collaboratively create, manage, and track physician contracts in order to have overview into all contracts, ensure compliance, and strategically plan for the future. Our software allows you to dynamically create contracts, keep track of changes, view past versions, manage the workflow of the contracts, and set custom alerts at key times in the contracts history.

How It Works

Our Simple Process

1

Create A Contract

Create a contract from scratch, or from previously created custom templates.
2

Add Initial Data

Add the initial data, such as the workflow (what departments/individuals will the contract get passed to), and the physician.
3

Pass Contract and Fill Out

Approve the contract, and pass it to another group to add addtional information. You can add addition amendments here, or just fill in the inputs of the contract.
4

Finish Contract

Once the contract has been completely filled out, the changes have been made, and the contract has been approved, all the parties can sign the contract and save it for good!

Features

Contract Manager does a lot!

Contracts

Create Contracts

Easily create contracts from previously made templates, paste in previous contracts, or create new ones from scratch. Just enter some basic contract information and the base contract will be created with ease.

Workflow

Set and Manage Workflow

Choose how the contract will flow through your organization. You can send the contracts to a department, or send them directly to individuals. Workflows can be saved and reused, or you can choose who to send the contract to once you are finished making edits.

Security

HIPAA Compliance and Security

Our application is served 100% over HTTPS using 256 bit end to end SSL encryption. We take security very seriously and are HIPAA compliant.

Analytics

Track Changes

All contract changes will be tracked with time stamps. This means you can compare contract versions, see who made changes, and view historical contracts.

Analytics

Advanced Analytics

Dig down into the details of your contracts, aggregate data, find contracts that are expiring soon, and much more.

Alerts

Real Time Alerts

Get notified of pending contracts, unsigned contracts, contracts soon to autorenew, contracts set to expire soon, and more!

General

Permission System

With our permission system, only those who should have access to the data, will have access. You can lock down who can view what in order to keep your data safe.

Download Data

It's your data--you can download all of it at any time!
