About Us

How We Got Started

Xxx was started by a healthcare professional working on a compliance project looking for software that fit all of the projects needs. While a few solutions existed in the market, there were none that covered all of the projects needs while also being affordable, user friendly, and easy to use. Xxxx was born out of this to provide a full package of healthcare compliance software that not only solved all of the compliance issues, but did so at a lower cost and with better results.

Our Philosophy

Simplicity

We strive to make everything we do as simple as possible for our customers. Our products are designed to be as user friendly as possible—we believe that the best software is software that people will enjoy using. By keeping things simple and intuitive, users of our products can get more done! We truly carry this philosophy through everything we do- from design, to pricing, to contract negotiation.

Security

Our phone and web applications are built with the highest security standards in mind. As a HIPAA compliant SAAS solution, we offer 256bit SSL end to end security. By delivering all of our data in a secure way, you don’t have to worry about data breaches and can sleep peacefully at night.

Cutting Edge Technology

Software moves fast, and xxxx is built on the latest technology. With the latest technology, we can quickly adapt to changing customer needs and quickly release new features. This also means our software is fast, scalable, and backedup. In fact, we built xxxx on Amazon AWS –the same service that NASA and Netflix use.
