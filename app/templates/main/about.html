About Us

Our Goal

Our goal is to provide the #1 physician arrangement and healthcare compliance software in the market and to continually improve what we offer!

How We Got Started

Baldur Health Care was started by a health care professional working on a physician arrangements compliance project looking for software that fit all of the projects needs. While a few solutions existed in the market, there were none that covered all of the requirements while also being affordable, modern, and easy to use. Baldur Health Care was born out of this frustration to provide a full package of health care compliance software that not only solved all of the compliance issues, but did so at a much lower cost, and with better results.
