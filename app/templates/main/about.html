About Us

Modern Physician Arrangement Software

Our Goal

Our goal is to provide the #1 physician arrangement and healthcare compliance software in the market and to continually get better! We truly believe that by helping others succeed, Baldur will succeed as well.

How We Got Started

Baldur Healthcare was started by a healthcare professional working on a physician arrangements compliance project looking for software that fit all of the projects needs. While a few solutions existed in the market, there were none that covered all of the projects needs while also being affordable, user friendly, and easy to use. Baldur Health Care was born out of this to provide a full package of healthcare compliance software that not only solved all of the compliance issues, but did so at a much lower cost and with better results.

Our Philosophy

We strive to make everything we do as simple as possible for our customers. Our products are designed to be user friendly—we believe that the best software is software that people will enjoy using.
